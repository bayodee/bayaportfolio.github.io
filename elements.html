Hospital Project Mandate

Project Mandate

This project aims at creating a website for a hospital, bridging the gap between Doctors and Patients. The platform will allow patients to:

  • Schedule appointments with doctors easily.
  • Purchase medical products and medications online.
  • Make payments directly through the website.
  • Pick up orders when visiting the hospital.

Doctors can also update their availability on the website, while senior management can track their daily schedules.

The Problem

Many patients face challenges visiting the hospital due to their busy schedules or ill health. This often results in:

  • Long wait times at hospitals.
  • Frustrating experiences due to multiple visits.
  • Delays in receiving essential care and medications.

The Solution

To solve these issues, our client aims to build a platform that makes doctor-patient interaction more convenient, efficient, and less tedious for both parties.


Key Features - Admin Portal

  • accounts

    Admin users can sign up, sign in, and sign out of the portal. They can create and manage their profiles. Only Super Admin users can grant access to other admin users.

  • client log

    The system will maintain a log of all clients who received services or purchased products. Admins can create, view, and edit client data, while only Super Admins can delete client profiles.

  • payments

    Payment records will be accessible to all admin staff, but only Super Admins can edit payment details.

  • Bookings and Appointments

    Admins can view all scheduled bookings and client appointments. The system will also send notifications and reminders for upcoming appointments.

  • Blog

    Admins can send out newsletters and upload or edit blog posts. Visitors can view all posts and updates from the blog feature.

  • FAQ's

    This feature manages frequently asked questions about the business process, product usage, and more. Admins can upload, edit, and delete data, while all users can view the FAQs.

Main Aim

  • To generate revenue for the hospital.
  • To simplify and enhance the patient experience.
  • To provide quality and affordable medical services.
  • To create better visibility and growth for the business.


Note: The focus is on building an MVP (minimum viable product) with essential functionalities.
